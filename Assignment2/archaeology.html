My Studies

Right now i am writing my bachelorthesis, where I focus on the analysis of human skeletal remains and their condition. This includes examining preservation, identifying key features, and interpreting biological and cultural information.

A central goal is to reconstruct aspects of life such as age, health, and possible circumstances of death based on physical evidence.

Key Areas

  • Skeletal analysis and identification
  • Preservation and condition assessment
  • Age and health estimation
  • Interpretation of burial contexts

Approach

My work combines careful observation with analytical reasoning to form evidence-based interpretations of past individuals and populations.
